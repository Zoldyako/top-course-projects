How to make Raspberry Truffle Brownies

This recipe comes from an old cookbook of mine that some women in my church put together years ago.

It’s one of my favorite cookbooks because so many of these women are fantastic cooks, and shared awesome recipes!

Whenever I’m wanting some new recipe inspiration, it’s one of the first I turn to.

  • 11/2 cups semi sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup salted butter
  • 3/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extrac
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Add chocolate chips and butter to a heavy saucepan over low heat. Stir constantly until melted and smooth. Remove from heat to cool slightly.
  3. In a mixing bowl beat to gather the brown sugar and eggs until well combined.
  4. Add melted chocolate mixture and vanilla and mix. Add flour and baking powder and mix.
  5. Spread batter evenly into the bottom of a lightly greased 8’’ or 9’’ square pan.
  6. Bake for 28-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the brownies comes out with few moist crumbs. Don’t overcook them or they won't be “fudgy”.

Milkshake

How to make a Chocolate Milkshake

Combine ice cream, milk, and chocolate sauce in a blender.

Blend until combined, add more milk if needed. For a thicker shake, add more ice cream. For a thinner shake, add more milk.

Serve topped with whipped cream, if desired

  • 6 cups vanilla ice cream or chocolate ice cream
  • 1/3 cup milk, or more as needed, or use chocolate milke
  • 1/3 cup chocolate sauce
  • whipped cream, optional, for topping

Steps

  1. Combine ice cream, milk and chocolate sauce in a blender and blend until combined.
  2. Top with whipped cream
